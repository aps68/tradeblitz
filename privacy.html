Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, log trades in your journal, use the AI Chat, or contact us for support. This may include your email address, trading metrics, API Keys (stored locally), and chat logs.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used primarily to provide and improve the TradeBlitz core services. We use your trade logs to compute performance metrics on your dashboard. Your chat histories may be processed by third-party AI providers (such as Google Gemini APIs) strictly for the purpose of generating conversational responses.

3. Data Storage and Security

Most of your data, including journal logs and API keys, are currently stored locally on your device using Browser Local Storage for maximum privacy. However, platform-wide metrics may occasionally be parsed by internal administrator accounts for overall performance audits.

4. Third-Party Services

When using the AI Chat feature, your messages are securely transmitted to Google's Gemini API infrastructure. Please review Google's privacy protocols regarding data passed through their generative AI APIs.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or your personal data, please contact us at krishnadevps123@gmail.com.
